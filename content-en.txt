Honey is a unique natural product, known since ancient times.

It has been described as a “priceless gift of nature".

Due to its unique healing properties on the human body, honey is generally recognized as a source of health, beauty and long life.

Bulgaria is the country where both climate and nature create excellent conditions for honey production. It is well-known and highly appreciated all over the world.
